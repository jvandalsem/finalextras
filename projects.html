Restaurant Review Site

My program allows users to search and save restaurants located in Ann Arbor, and gets these restaurants' data from the Yelp Fusion API. Users then have the option to do a few other things with these searched and saved restaurants. ALL users can view ALL restaurants saved from ALL users, along with the rating and price range of the restaurant(from Yelp API). Registered and logged in users can make a collection of restaurants, and can include/select all previously saved restaurants. Registered and logged in users can make a review about a restaurant/experience/meal, and sequentially rate the review on a scale from 1-5. Registered and logged in users can then view their own collections and reviews, and perform changes to both. Registered and logged in users can delete their own collections, and can update the rating of a restaurant review.

NFL Weather and Odds Analysis

My project​​ incorporates a dataset of NFL games that contains odds, scores, and weather from 1966 through the current 2018 season, and an NFL API from Sports Radar that gathers loads of live data from the NFL which also includes fields like games, odds, scores, and weather, from the years 2001-2018. My goal​​ was to join the two sets of data, to try and accurately determine NFL team regular season performances in specific weather conditions, based off of my own categorization of weather types. I will then use this data to see how often each team is ‘favored’ or chosen to win, prior to playing in each of these game conditions. I am looking at this comparison, because weather is usually a predicted variable, like choosing a team winner.
