NFL Weather and Odds Analysis

My project​​ incorporates a dataset of NFL games that contains odds, scores, and weather from 1966 through the current 2018 season, and an NFL API from Sports Radar that gathers loads of live data from the NFL which includes fields like games, odds, scores, and weather, from the years 2001-2018. My goal​​ was to join the two sets of data, to try and accurately determine NFL team regular season performances in specific weather conditions, based off of my own categorization of weather types.
